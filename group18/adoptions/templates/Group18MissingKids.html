What should I do if I can't find my child...

  • Look carefully and ask the people around you for help. Pay attention to the nearby intersection when you get lost. The child may be nearby.

  • Keep a cool head when your child gets lost.

  • If the child is lost while visiting the supermarket, seek help from the supermarket staff and service center immediately.

  • When going out to play, children are easy to get lost in the crowd, please call the police.

  • Seek help from netizens, publish the "missing notice" through the Internet, and ask more friends on the Internet to help forward and spread it.
